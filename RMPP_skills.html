SWOT Analysis

I employ the SWOT analysis framework as part of my self-evaluation and strategic planning processes. SWOT is an acronym that stands for Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. It has enabled me to critically assess my current situation and gain a better awareness of myself and my possibilities. By identifying my strengths and limitations, I can maximize my skills and focus on areas that need development. Furthermore, the analysis provides useful insights into the opportunities that are accessible to me, as well as the potential challenges that may hamper my growth.
